About Us

Aggie Web Developers is an on-campus organization dedicated to empowering Aggies to build technical prowess in web development, network with peers and industry contacts, and learn more about careers in the technology industry. Our primary mission is to develop the next generation of professionals, entrepreneurs, and hobbyists; by providing students with an opportunity for growth in web development skills that are ethical and just.
